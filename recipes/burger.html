Mexican turkey burger

Mexican turkey burger

Inspired by Mexican tortillas and American Burgers

Preperation time is 20 minutes and cooking time is 25 minutes.

Serving is for 6 people

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 medium green bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1 can whole kernel corn, drained
  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1 package taco seasoning mix
  • 1/3 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 6 tortillas
  • 6 tablespoons sour cream
  • 2 cups shredded lettuce

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat oven to 230 degrees C. Coat a medium baking dish with cooking spray

  2. Step 2

    3. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat, and saute the onion, green pepper and garlic 5 minutes. Remove from heat and cool slightly

  3. Step 3

    4. In a small bowl mix the salsa and 1/2 corn. In a large bowl mix the onion mixture with turkey, taco seasoning and 2 tablespoons of the salsa mixture. Divide into 6 patties, press into the breadcrumbs to lightly coat on all sides. Arrange coated patties in the prepared baking dish.

  4. Step 4

    5. Bake the patties 10 minutes in the preheated oven. Drain any liquid from the dish, turn patties and spread with the remaining salsa mixture. Continue baking for 10 minutes to an internal T of 75 degrees C.

  5. Step 5

    6. Warm the tortillas in the microwave, about 30 seconds on high. Wrap the cooked turkey patties in the warmed tortillas with sour cream and lettuce. Sprinkle with remaining corn to serve.
