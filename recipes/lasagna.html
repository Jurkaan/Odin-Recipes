Enchilada Lasagna

Perfect Enchilada lasagna

This is a recipe for perfect Enchilada lasagna.

Preperation time is 20 minutes and cooking time is 1 hour 30 minutes

Serving is for 8 persons

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1,25 pounds ground turkey
  • 1 (28 ounce) can enchilada sauce
  • 1 (14,5 ounce) can diced tomatoes with lime juice and cilantro
  • 1 (16 ounce) package small-curd cottage cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 5 corn tortillas, halved
  • 2 cups shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • cooking spray
  • 1 green onion, diced

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and garlic; cook and stir until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir ground turkey; cook until no longer pink, about 5 minutes. Drain excess grease.

  2. Step 2

    3. Stir enchilada sauce and diced tomatoes into the pot. Simmer until flavors combine, about 20 minutes. Remove from heat

  3. Step 3

    4. Preheat the oven to 190 degrees C

  4. Step 4

    5. Mix cottage cheese, egg and cumin in a small bowl

  5. Step 5

    6. Spread 1/3 of the turkey sauce in the bottom of and 8-inch baking dish. Cover with 1/2 of the corn tortillas. Spread 1/2 of the cottge cheese mixture on top. Sprinkle 1/3 of the Mexican cheese on top. Repeat layers once more, ending with remaining turkey sauce and Mexican cheese.

  6. Step 6

    7. Spray a large piece of aluminium foil with cooking spray; cover the baking dish with aluminium foil

  7. Step 7

    8. Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly, about 30 minutes. Remove aluminium foil and continue baking until top is browned, about 15 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes before servings. Garnish with green onion
